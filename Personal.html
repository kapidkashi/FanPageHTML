Real Name:   Arthur Madrigalejos Nery
Birthday: 28 January, 1997
Nationality: Filipino
Born: Philippines

 Arthur Madrigalejos Nery also known as Arthur Nery is a Filipino singer,
actor, model and social media influencer. Arthur Nery is the brother of filmmaker
and scriptwriter Orpheus. Arthur Nery was an actor with Oro Teatro Bulawanon,
a theatre group based in Cagayan De Oro. Chinggo to family and friends,
first realized that he wanted to be a singer when he was six years old.

His first public performance happened at a cousin's debut.

Singing Career


 Arthur Nery was an actor with Oro Teatro Bulawanon, a theatre group based in Cagayan De Oro City.
He was given an opportunity to compete at the at the World Championship of Performing Arts (WCOPA)
in the United States but he failed to secure money for his travel expenses.

In 2018, Arthur Nery quit his job as a call center agent to focus on his singing career.
That year, he released his first single, Life Puzzle, through the help of 9K Records.

Arthur Nery is now a talent of O/C Records, a music company owned and managed by singer Kean Cipriano.
Prior to the onset of the pandemic, he was busy performing in different music venues and bars.
